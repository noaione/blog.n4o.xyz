## Admonitions

How to use:

```jsx
<Admonition type="warning" title="Do not do this">
  Admonition Content
</Admonition>
```

Type: "`warning`", "`tip`", "`caution`", "`note`", "`important`", "`question`"

  peepee tip
</Admonition>
